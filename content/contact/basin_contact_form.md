## What I Can Help With

- **Tool Restoration:** Questions about restoring vintage hand planes, saws, and other traditional woodworking tools
- **Woodworking Techniques:** Traditional joinery, hand tool techniques, and furniture making
- **Workshop Setup:** Advice on organizing and equipping a hand tool workshop
- **Project Guidance:** Suggestions for your woodworking projects
- **Tool Recommendations:** Finding quality vintage or new hand tools

Feel free to share photos of your tools or projects in your message!
